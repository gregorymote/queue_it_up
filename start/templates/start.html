Queuenopsis

Oh, sup thanks for hanging at Queue it Up, the chillest way for the whole fam to curate vibes of a kickback that’s bound to launch into a rager.

Here’s the basic gist:
  • Start a Party by logging into your Spotify Premium account and selecting a device connected to your opened and logged in Spotify Account
  • Let fam join your party by sharing your unique party code
  • Once you start your party, each partier takes a turn picking/customizing a song category
  • After the partier submits a category, then each partier gets to add a song to the queue that fits the category
  • When all partiers submit their songs to the queue, the songs in the queue will play on the selected device and a new partier gets to choose a category for the next round
  • After the first round kicks off, the rest of the rounds seamlessly flow together
  • During each round, throw fam some likes and view whose song was totes flames that round
and that's it...
