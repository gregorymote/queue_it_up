About Logging In

What is this?

Queue it Up requires the party host to have access to a Spotify Premium account. After selecting the Log in button, you will be brought to Spotify's official Log in page.

If you are not redirected to Spotify's login page after submitting your username and are brought to the 'terms and conditions' page, it is because your web browser remembers you. If you want to log in with a different account, try clearing your web browser's cookies and site data.
